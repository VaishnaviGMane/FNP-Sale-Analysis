 Sales Analysis Dashboard (FNP)
Project Overview
This project delivers an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard designed to provide leadership with a clear, data-driven view of business performance across time, products, occasions, and geography. The dashboard consolidates order, revenue, customer spending, and delivery performance metrics to support strategic decision-making, demand planning, and marketing optimization.
________________________________________
Key Business Metrics (High-Level Performance)
•	Total Orders: 1,000
•	Total Revenue: ₹35.21 Lakhs
•	Average Order-to-Delivery Time: 5.53 hours
•	Average Customer Spend: ₹3,520.98
These KPIs indicate healthy customer spending behavior and efficient order fulfillment within a short delivery window.
________________________________________
Revenue Insights
1. Revenue by Occasion
•	Anniversary, Raksha Bandhan, and Holi emerge as the top revenue-generating occasions.
•	Diwali and Valentine’s Day contribute comparatively lower revenue, indicating potential opportunities for targeted promotions or product bundling during these periods.
•	Occasion-based filtering enables focused analysis of festive demand patterns.
2. Revenue by Category
•	Colors and Sweets are the highest-performing categories, significantly driving overall revenue.
•	Soft Toys and Cakes show strong and consistent demand.
•	Categories like Mugs and Plants contribute less, suggesting scope for cross-selling or repositioning.
________________________________________
Time-Based Performance Analysis
3. Revenue by Hour (Order Time)
•	Peak revenue is observed during evening and late-night hours, highlighting customer preference for placing orders later in the day.
•	Mid-day fluctuations suggest potential to optimize promotions during low-traffic hours.
4. Revenue by Month
•	Revenue spikes are visible in February, August, and November, aligning with major festive and celebratory seasons.
•	Lower revenue months highlight opportunities for off-season campaigns and discounts to stabilize cash flow.
________________________________________
Product & Regional Performance
5. Top 5 Products by Revenue
•	Magnam Set, Quia Gift, and Dolores Gift are the top revenue-generating products.
•	These products can be prioritized for inventory planning, featured promotions, and premium bundles.
6. Top 10 Cities by Orders
•	Imphal, Dhanbad, and Kavali lead in order volume.
•	Regional demand insights can guide logistics optimization, warehouse placement, and city-specific marketing strategies.
________________________________________
Interactivity & Decision Support
•	Dynamic filters for Delivery Date, Order Date, and Occasion allow stakeholders to drill down into specific periods or events.
•	The dashboard enables quick identification of trends, outliers, and performance gaps without requiring technical expertise.
________________________________________
Business Impact & Recommendations
•	Improve revenue in low-performing occasions (Diwali, Valentine’s Day) through targeted offers and personalized marketing.
•	Leverage peak ordering hours with flash deals and optimized delivery staffing.
•	Focus inventory and promotions on high-performing products and cities to maximize ROI.
•	Use seasonal insights to plan marketing calendars and demand forecasting.
________________________________________
Conclusion
This Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive, actionable view of business performance, enabling management to make informed decisions across sales strategy, marketing, operations, and customer engagement. It transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights that directly support revenue growth and operational efficiency.
